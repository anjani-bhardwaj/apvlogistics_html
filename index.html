Flexibility

Logistics often require a high degree of flexibility to adapt to changing circumstances or unforeseen challenges that could impact delivery times. This might involve rerouting shipments, adjusting schedules, or deploying alternative transportation methods to ensure on-time delivery.

Tracking and Monitoring

Shipments often come with advanced tracking and monitoring systems to provide real-time updates on the status and location of the shipment. This allows for better visibility and control over the delivery process.

Priority Handling

Expedited shipments are typically given priority over standard shipments. This might involve dedicating specific resources, such as vehicles or transportation routes, exclusively to expedited orders.
